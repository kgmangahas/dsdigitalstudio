I’m an engineer, designer and brand stylist based in Manila, Philippines. I’m super passionate about colors, graphics, and typography and I want to use this gift to help entrepreneurs like you grow your business online.

Before launching my online business, I’ve worked as a Process Data Engineer in the oil and gas industry. But I felt something lacking - I was looking for more than the usual 9 to 5. I found my calling in working with female solopreneurs - helping them get past the overwhelm of doing their business alone so they can focus on their area of brilliance

As a business owner myself, I can understand that starting a business is such a massive leap. Without the proper support system, the stress can be overpowering.
